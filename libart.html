Cultural Arts


The National University of Singapore (NUS) plays a pivotal role in enriching the arts and culture landscape in Singapore and the region. Through its diverse programs in the arts, humanities, and social sciences, NUS fosters interdisciplinary collaboration and encourages creative expression among students and faculty. The university hosts numerous events, exhibitions, and performances that highlight local and international talents, promoting cultural exchange and understanding. Additionally, NUS's initiatives, such as the NUS Museum and various cultural research projects, contribute to the preservation and promotion of Singapore’s heritage, ensuring that the vibrant tapestry of the nation’s arts and culture continues to thrive and evolve.
